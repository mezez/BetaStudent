BETASTUDENT INFO

This app is designed to meet the basic needs of students and make their study life much easier. You can add timetables for lectures and exams, add courses, projects and assignments. Reminders will be set for submission deadlines of assignments and projects. Reminders will also be set for exam dates. You can also add links to your favourite educational apps and access them from the app. Note that you can slide list items from right to left to edit and delete. Amazing innit?
